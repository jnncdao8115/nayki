About Nike

Our Heritage

Nike, Inc. is an American multinational corporation that is engaged in the design, development, manufacturing, and worldwide marketing and sales of footwear, apparel, equipment, accessories, and services. Founded in 1964, Nike has grown to become one of the most recognized brands in the world, inspiring athletes and consumers alike with innovative products and compelling stories.

Our Mission

To bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete* in the world. (*If you have a body, you are an athlete.) We strive to create products that empower people to reach their full potential, regardless of their sport or activity.

Sustainability & Innovation

At Nike, sustainability is at the core of our innovation. We are committed to reducing our environmental impact through sustainable materials, ethical manufacturing practices, and initiatives aimed at preserving our planet for future generations.

Our Community

Nike actively engages with communities worldwide, supporting youth programs, sports initiatives, and social causes. We believe in the power of sport to unite, inspire, and uplift individuals everywhere.
